Prompt Recall: Your AI Prompt Companion

Prompt Recall is a powerful browser extension designed to streamline your workflow with AI models. Capture, save, and reuse your favorite prompts with ease, right in your browser.

Key Features:

- Save and Organize Prompts: Effortlessly save any text as a prompt. Assign a title and tags to keep your prompts organized and easy to find.
- Quick Access and Reuse: Your saved prompts are just a click away. Copy them into any text field with a single click.
- Powerful Search and Filtering:
    - Full-Text Search: Search through the title, content, and tags of your prompts.
    - Tag Filtering: Click on a tag to instantly filter your prompts.
- Smart Sorting: Sort your prompts by most recent, most used, last used, or alphabetically.
- Markdown and Code Formatting: Your prompts are beautifully formatted with Markdown and syntax highlighting for code snippets, making them easy to read and understand.
- "Show More" for Long Prompts: Long prompts are elegantly truncated, with a "Show More" button to reveal the full content without cluttering your view.
- Clipboard Integration: When creating a new prompt, the extension automatically offers to pre-fill the content from your clipboard.
- Import/Export: Easily back up your prompts or transfer them to another device with the import and export feature.

Prompt Recall is the perfect tool for anyone who frequently interacts with AI models and wants to keep their prompts organized and readily accessible.
