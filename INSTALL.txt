QUICK INSTALLATION GUIDE
========================

Prompt Recall - Chrome Extension

STEP 1: Open Chrome Extensions
-------------------------------
1. Open Google Chrome
2. Type in address bar: chrome://extensions/
3. Press Enter

STEP 2: Enable Developer Mode
------------------------------
1. Look for "Developer mode" toggle in top-right corner
2. Click to enable it

STEP 3: Load Extension
----------------------
1. Click "Load unpacked" button (top-left area)
2. Navigate to and select this folder: prompt-recall
3. Click "Select Folder"

STEP 4: You're Done!
--------------------
The extension icon should now appear in your Chrome toolbar.
If you don't see it:
- Click the puzzle piece icon (extensions menu)
- Find "Prompt Recall"
- Click the pin icon to keep it visible

USAGE:
------
- Click the extension icon to open your prompt library
- Click "+ New Prompt" to add a prompt
- Or right-click selected text and choose "Save as AI Prompt"
- Use Copy or Paste buttons to use your prompts

KEYBOARD SHORTCUT:
------------------
Press Ctrl+Shift+P (Cmd+Shift+P on Mac) to open the extension popup
